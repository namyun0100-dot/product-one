1. 10-day flow snapshot

One-line summary: Not bigger gains, but fewer leaks.

3. Practical checklist

1) Write a 10-day spending ceiling

A short cap line is more effective than a complex budget. Keep it simple and visible.

2) Cancel one unused subscription

Even a small recurring fee unlocks mental space. Less noise, cleaner flow.

3) Keep five receipts

Patterns show up quickly. Once you see the loop, it becomes easy to cut.
